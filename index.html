Streamer Start

Empowering New Voices an innovative and supportive program designed to help new streamers establish and grow their audience through effective newsletter marketing. By offering a subsidized, low-cost service, StreamerStart aims to provide the necessary tools and resources to new streamers who may lack the financial means or technical knowledge to create and manage professional newsletters. Our Pricing

$9.99/month for the first 6 months,
then $19.99/month for the next 6 months.

- Streamers with less than 1,000 followers/subscribers
- Streaming for less than 1 year
- Limited to 100 participants initially (to manage resources)

Features:

Basic Newsletter Setup:
- Simple, customizable template
- Up to 250 subscribers
- Monthly newsletter
Growth Tools:
- Basic subscriber analytics
- Simple guide on growing subscriber list
Community Support:
- Access to a private Discord channel for program participants
- Monthly group coaching call with a newsletter expert
Educational Resources:
- Access to a library of basic email marketing tutorials
- Monthly webinar on newsletter best practices for streamers
Graduation Path:
- Clear guidelines on transitioning to standard plans as the streamer grows

Application Process:

- Simple online form explaining their streaming journey and goals
- Brief statement on why they need this support
- Proof of streaming activity and follower count

Program Benefits:

For Streamers:
- Affordable access to professional newsletter tools
- Support in building their community
- Learning essential marketing skills
For Your Business:
- Building brand loyalty with potential future customers
- Positive brand image in the streaming community
- Potential for case studies and success stories
For the Streaming Community:
- Supporting diversity in content creation
- Helping new voices reach their audience

Implementation:


- Create a dedicated landing page for the program
- Promote through streaming communities and forums
- Partner with small streamer support groups or platforms
- Offer referral bonuses to existing clients who recommend eligible streamers

About The Program

This pilot program allows you to support new streamers while also building a pipeline of future customers. As these streamers grow, they can transition to your standard plans, having already experienced the value of your service. Remember to clearly communicate the terms, including the price increase after 6 months and 12 months, to ensure transparency. Also, consider implementing a simple review process at these milestones to ensure the program continues to serve those most in need.

Overview

Welcome to StreamerStart, a subsidized, low-cost newsletter service specifically designed for new streamers with limited resources.
Our goal is to empower new voices in the streaming community by providing affordable access to professional newsletter tools and support.
